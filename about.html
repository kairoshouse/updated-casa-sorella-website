The Firm

Our Identity

A specialized alternative for the modern resident who recognizes that the integrity of their space is not the place to cut corners.

Casa Sorella Translates to Sister's House.

We operate on a simple, foundational principle: we treat your residence with the technical care and protective discretion we would provide for our own. In an industry of high-turnover platforms and unverified labor, Casa Sorella exists as a specialized alternative for the modern resident who recognizes that the integrity of their space is not the place to cut corners.

We provide the efficiency of a quick-service visit backed by the technical authority of a licensed GC firm. Most of our projects — from TV mounting to gallery arrangements — are completed in under two hours. However, that time is supported by a professional understanding of residential architecture.

Whether your walls are metal-stud, masonry, or traditional wood-frame, we verify the composition of the substrate before the first hole is drilled. We don't rely on guesswork; we ensure every installation is permanent, level, and correct.

Our Position

We are a firm built for those who prioritize their time and their sanctuary. We offer a professionalized approach where competence is the baseline, not the goal.

Structural Integrity

We utilise project-specific, structural-grade hardware tailored to your building's specific construction.

Building Liaison

As a licensed and insured firm, we provide the documented accountability required by luxury high-rise management, ensuring your space — and your neighbor's — is protected.

Site Discretion

Our process is designed for high-value residences. We provide a quiet, methodical, and consistent presence that respects the environment we are invited into.

The Standard of Care

Our Operating Principles

01
The Walkthrough

We begin every project by listening. Every space is unique, and every vision requires a specific technical approach. We walk through the placement, confirm the substrate, and ensure the plan aligns with your expectations before the first tool is drawn.

02
Professional Discretion

We recognise that we are entering your sanctuary, not a construction site. Our presence is designed to be quiet, methodical, and respectful. We arrive on time, maintain a clean workspace, and exit with the same level of professional conduct we would bring to our own homes.

03
Technical Competence

In our firm, competence is the baseline. We bring the structural knowledge of a licensed GC to every residential task. We don't guess at wall composition; we identify the metal, wood, or masonry architecture of your building to ensure every anchor is permanent and secure.

04
Standardised Rates

Efficiency should not come with a hidden cost. We provide fixed-project quotes based on the scope of work, not the hours on a clock. This eliminates the hourly unknown and ensures you receive a locked-in price before we arrive.

05
Firm Accountability

We are a specialised firm, not a third-party platform. You have direct access to your partner-installer from the first quote to the final walkthrough. We carry comprehensive liability insurance tailored for luxury high-rise environments, providing the documented protection you and your building management require.
