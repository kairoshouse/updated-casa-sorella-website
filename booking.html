What to include

A brief message is all it takes. Include these four things and we'll have a fixed quote back to you quickly.

Your message should cover
  1. Your name
  2. Your property address
  3. What you're looking for — TV mounting, art installation, furniture, or a combination
  4. An idea on timing

A note on timing — We suggest scheduling about a week after move-in, once you've had time to live in the space and figure out exactly where you want everything. It's a lot easier to move a couch a few inches now than once the TV is mounted.
